# Campaign Atlas — TEMPLATE

This atlas is the human-readable view of `repos.yaml`. It is generated, not
hand-written: run `warroom setup` or `warroom maps assign --write` to produce
`maps/campaign-atlas.md` from your manifest. `campaign-atlas.md` is gitignored
so your project-specific map never travels upstream.

The generated file lists every mapped repo, its Sergeant, and specialist
context. Hand-written notes added between the `warroom:notes` markers are
preserved across regeneration.
